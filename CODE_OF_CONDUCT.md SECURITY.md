# Code of Conduct  
## Professional and Governance-Aligned Participation  

---

## Purpose

This repository documents a structured AI System Governance architecture for high-risk domains.

Discussions, contributions, and reviews must remain:

- Professional
- Evidence-based
- Respectful
- Governance-focused

This is a safety governance repository, not a debate forum.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Participants are expected to:

- Engage respectfully and constructively
- Focus on governance structure and safety logic
- Provide evidence-backed arguments
- Maintain professional tone
- Respect severity enforcement principles
- Preserve structural integrity of the framework

Disagreement is acceptable. Disrespect is not.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following are not permitted:

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Dismissive or inflammatory language
- Attempts to weaken safety boundaries for convenience
- Minimising high-risk exposure without evidence
- Misrepresentation of governance logic
- Off-topic discussions unrelated to system governance

Safety governance requires disciplined discourse.

---

## Governance Discussions

Debate regarding:

- Severity thresholds
- Escalation logic
- Risk classification
- Monitoring sensitivity
- Framework evolution

Must remain structured and documentation-based.

Opinions must be supported by governance reasoning.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Comment moderation
- Contribution rejection
- Access restriction (if applicable)

Governance integrity takes precedence over participation volume.

---

## Final Principle

- This repository represents structured AI Safety Governance.
- Professional conduct preserves clarity, credibility, and structural discipline.
- All participants are expected to uphold that standard.
